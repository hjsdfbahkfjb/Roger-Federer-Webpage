Welcome!

Roger Federer is a Swedish tennis player who has been devoted to the sport since he was eight years old. His love for tennis is what allowed him to become so good. Federer was a gifted player since his childhood and he dominated the junior league. Soon enough, Feder became a pro tennis player and started to climb the rankings. At one point in his career, he was the best in the world, and it was like nobody could stop him. Although, his reign came to an end in 2022 when he retired from the sport of tennis. Federer will always be remembered as one of the greatest tennis players of all time.
